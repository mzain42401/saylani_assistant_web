Services

Saylani Welfare Trust is one of the largest NGO in Pakistan, Saylani's mission is to support the underprivileged by providing essential services such as free food, ration distribution, medical assistance, clean water, and education. Our educational initiatives encompass schools, vocational training programs, and modern IT education. As an affiliate of Saylani Welfare International Trust in Pakistan, which has been dedicated to humanitarian service since 1999, we contribute significantly to the well-being of society, investing over a billion rupees monthly in various philanthropic endeavors.

Health

Medical clinics, vaccinations, healthcare facilities, ambulance services, free medical camps, disease prevention programs, and health education initiatives.

Education

Schools, scholarships, vocational training, adult literacy programs, educational materials, tuition assistance, and digital learning resources.

Sadiqah Jariah

Sustainable charity projects, livelihood support, community development initiatives, Zakat distribution, economic empowerment programs.

Social Welfare

Orphan care, elderly support, widows' assistance, shelter homes, rehabilitation programs, family counseling, and advocacy for social justice.

Disastar Aid

Emergency relief, food distribution, temporary shelters, medical assistance, clean water provision, psychological support, and disaster preparedness training.

Saylani Ahsaas

Poverty alleviation efforts, food distribution, clothing drives, microfinance projects, livelihood support, and community empowerment programs.
